About Rajshree

A Legacy Built on Care, Skill & Discipline

Since 2008, Rajshree College of Nursing has trained thousands of dedicated nurses who now serve in leading hospitals across India and abroad. Our curriculum blends INC-aligned academics with intensive hands-on clinical exposure, so every graduate steps into the ward ready — not just qualified.

We believe nursing is a calling as much as a career, and our faculty, facilities, and hospital partnerships are all built around one goal: producing confident, compassionate, clinically sharp professionals.

Our Mission

To deliver nursing education of the highest clinical and ethical standard, nurturing skilled, empathetic caregivers ready for real-world healthcare.

Our Vision

To be recognised as a leading nursing institution in Central India, known for producing globally employable, compassionate healthcare leaders.

Why Choose Rajshree

  • INC & State Nursing Council approved programs
  • Tie-ups with 12+ multi-specialty hospitals for clinical training
  • Dedicated placement cell with 96% placement success
  • Experienced, PhD & M.Sc qualified nursing faculty

Programs Offered

Courses Designed for Real Clinical Careers

Every program combines classroom learning with structured hospital postings from year one, so theory always meets practice.

GNM

General Nursing & Midwifery — a comprehensive diploma building strong clinical fundamentals for hospital-ready nurses.

Duration: 3.5 Years (incl. internship) Eligibility: 10+2 (any stream), 40% aggregate
Learn More

B.Sc Nursing

A degree program building advanced clinical, leadership, and research competencies for a lifelong nursing career.

Duration: 4 Years Eligibility: 10+2 with PCB, 45% aggregate
Learn More

Post Basic B.Sc Nursing

A career-advancement degree for GNM diploma holders seeking specialised clinical and management roles.

Duration: 2 Years Eligibility: GNM with registered RNRM
Learn More

ANM

Auxiliary Nurse Midwifery — a foundational program focused on community health and basic maternal care.

Duration: 2 Years Eligibility: 10+2 (any stream), 35% aggregate
Learn More
Campus Life

World-Class Facilities for Real Learning

A campus built around hospital-realistic training, comfortable living, and everything a student needs to focus on becoming an exceptional nurse.

Modern Labs

Fully-equipped nursing foundation, pre-clinical & midwifery labs with mannequins and simulators.

Smart Classrooms

Digital-enabled classrooms with audio-visual aids for interactive, up-to-date learning.

Library

A comprehensive collection of nursing texts, journals, and digital resources for research.

Computer Lab

High-speed systems supporting digital literacy, exams, and research training.

Hostel

Secure, supervised residential hostels with hygienic dining and 24x7 warden support.

Clinical Training

Structured hospital postings across 12+ partner hospitals from the first year onward.

Experienced Faculty

M.Sc & PhD qualified nursing educators with years of clinical and teaching experience.

Placement Assistance

Dedicated placement cell connecting graduates with hospitals across India and abroad.

Transportation

Safe, scheduled bus services connecting campus with major city routes.
